- Vitest: 1 FAILED — stale test `test/b20.test.ts:121` expects 9,500-block chunks; impl `lib/report.ts:57` uses 1,900 → 11 chunks. Update test.

- Explore board + /coin trade view render fabricated market data (lib/launchpad-mock.ts) while page copy claims "Every token launched through LaunchB20, trading on its bonding curve."
